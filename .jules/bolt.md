## 2025-05-15 - Optimizing Terminal Keystrokes Re-rendering
**Learning:** In the `Terminal` component, mapping over an array of historical command structures directly inside the `Terminal` main function causes expensive deep re-rendering of all historical items and the standalone `TerminalClock` component upon every user keystroke. Attaching a `ref` within the `.map` iteration also creates redundant and problematic assignment loops on each keystroke.
**Action:** Extract large iterative list items (`TerminalCommandItem`) and un-associated sub-components (`TerminalClock`) into their own `React.memo` components. Ensure single refs (e.g. `terminalRef` for scrolling to bottom) are only attached to a static element at the bottom of the container rather than mapped dynamically inside the loop.
